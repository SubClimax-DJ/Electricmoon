Our Story

Electric Moon creates live light and laser shows that elevate bands beyond the stage — into full-blown sonic galaxies. Born under the stars and raised by the waves, our lighting is fueled by rhythm, color, and the raw energy of live music.

We’re not just flipping switches — we’re crafting atmospheres. From folk-inspired sets to extended jam sessions, our lighting evolves with the music, amplifying every crescendo with immersive visuals, swirling beams, and lunar-level vibes.

Whether you're a band looking to trip the lights fantastic or a venue chasing that extra spark, Electric Moon is here to drift, pulse, and launch your live show into orbit.

Venue Lighting

Electric Moon doesn’t just light up bands — we light up the whole space. Whether it’s a gritty rock stage, an intimate listening room, or a polished ballroom, we bring immersive lighting design tailored to the mood, energy, and heartbeat of your event.

We handle everything from custom stage setups and synchronized light shows to ambient uplighting and full-room transformations. Our gear is pro-grade. Our touch is artistic. Our goal? To make every performance and party unforgettable.

From soundcheck to encore, from first dance to final toast — we’ve got the lights that move with the music and match the moment.

Let Electric Moon bring your venue to life with beams, color, texture, and motion. Because when the lights are right, the vibe is unstoppable.
