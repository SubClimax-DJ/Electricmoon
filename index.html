Our Story

Electric Moon is a folk-rooted, jam-blasted band born under the stars and raised by the waves. Fronted by an Italian-American surfer, skater, and guitar shredder with a soul full of reverb, the band weaves acoustic melodies into full-blown electric journeys — complete with light shows, lasers, and lunar-level vibes.

Think campfire harmonies that morph into galactic improvisation. Think sunsets on the coast turning into midnight fuzz storms. Think surfboards, skate decks, and sonic exploration.

From the street to the stage, from the sand to the stars — Electric Moon is here to drift, jam, and launch.
